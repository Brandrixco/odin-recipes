Peanut Butter Cookies

Yummy fun for the Whole Family

An easy to make cookie that is both sweet and salty while being wonderfully soft to chew

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1 cup peanut butter
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 large egg
  • 3/4 tsp baking powder

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350F.
  2. whisk flour and baking powder together.
  3. Using a mixer combine and mix butter, brown sugar and sugar together with a pinch of salt.
  4. Add peanut butter to the mix.
  5. Mix in egg and vanilla extra then add flour mixture and beat until incororated.
  6. Roll dough into one inch balls and place on baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  7. Flatten cookies with a fork in a criss-cross pattern.
  8. Bake cookies for about 10 minutes.
  9. Allow cookies to cool completely on baking sheet.
