Chicken Parmesan

Classic Italian Favourite

Breaded chicken breast topped up with mozzarella cheese, paramesan cheese

rich tomato sauce and served with spaghetti marinara

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 pound of chicken breast fillets
  • 1 cup of shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 can of crushed tomatoes
  • Basil leaves
  • Ground pepper
  • 1 1/2 cups of Panko bread crumbs
  • 1/2 tsp Garlic powder
  • 1/4 cup of Grated parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 cup of all purpose flower
  • Kosher salt
  • 4 cloves of sliced garlic
  • 2 tbsp of Extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 large eggs

Steps

  1. Pre-heat oven at 400F. In a bowl mix the panko breading, garlic powder and parmesan. Mix the breading base with the eggs flour. Season the chicken with salt and pepper than coat each piece of chicken in flour while shaking off the excess. Dip the chicken in the egg mixture and than dip the coated chicken breast in the bread crumb mix. Do so with all the pieces of chicken
  2. Meanwhile, make marinara: In a medium pot over medium heat, heat oil. Add onion and garlic and cook until soft, 4 minutes. Add in tomatoes and water, season with salt, pepper, and red pepper flakes, and let simmer, 10 minutes. Remove from heat and stir in parsley.
  3. Pour sauce into a 9"-x-13" baking dish and place chicken in sauce. Top with mozzarella and bake until cheese is melty, 10 to 12 minutes. If desired, broil until cheese is golden, 3 minutes.
  4. Garnish with parsley and serve immediately.
